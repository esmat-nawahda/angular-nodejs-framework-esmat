Job seekers can add posts in which they may present their expertises, projects or publish valuable information to benefit other members.

Registered companies can post different jobs where can reach all the participated students. Candidates with a similarity greater than 70% with the job requirements will be notified of this job and it will be added to their job lists and automatically their CVs will be sent to the advertiser of this job.

Everyone in the system can add events. If anyone has an interview or a qualification exam for example, he can create a new event providing it with a specific time. And then, an alert will notify him for reminding.

A great task for the system is matching the different posted jobs with candidates. A semantic search for the job requirements and candidates' skills, and an auto evaluation provide job seekers with the ability to know themselves and their qualifications. Where, the system gives each a percentage of compatibility from hundred.

KitJobz is a social system in which it creates an interactive environment between both companies and graduated students. The system allow them communicate with each others by commenting and discussing on posts and jobs. Also jobseekers can send each other messages.

Job seekers don't need to create account. All they need to do is login using their Linkedin accounts.
